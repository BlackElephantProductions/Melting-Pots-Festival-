About the Festival

The Melting Pots of Indigenous Arts Festival is a 9-day celebration of traditional and contemporary indigenous arts in South Africa. It features performances, exhibitions, storytelling, workshops, and more to preserve and showcase the richness of our cultural heritage.

Call for Applications

We are looking for dancers, musicians, poets, visual artists, traditional storytellers, and cultural facilitators. To apply, send your biography, proposal, past work samples, and availability to: submissions@blackelephantprod.co.za

Deadline: 15 September 2025
